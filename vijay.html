Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar, known as Thalapathy Vijay, is a celebrated Indian actor, playback singer, and politician, primarily associated with Tamil cinema. Early Life and Education Vijay was born on 22 June 1974 in Madras (now Chennai), Tamil Nadu, India to film director S. A. Chandrasekhar and playback singer Shoba Chandrasekhar. He had a younger sister, Vidhya, who passed away at the age of two. Vijay attended Fathima Matriculation Higher Secondary School and Balalok School in Chennai. He later enrolled in Loyola College for a Bachelor’s in Visual Communication but dropped out to focus on his acting career.
